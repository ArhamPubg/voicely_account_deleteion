Introduction

At Voicely AI, we value your privacy and give you full control over your data. Users of Voicely AI can permanently delete their account and all associated personal data.

How to Delete Your Account

Follow these simple steps within the Voicely AI app to submit an account deletion request:

  1. Open the app.
  2. Navigate to Profile → Settings.
  3. Tap "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

Data Deleted

When you request to delete your account, the following information is permanently removed:

  • User profile information
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • User preferences and settings
  • User-generated content associated with the account

Data Retention

Please note that some information may be retained for up to 30 days for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes before permanent deletion. After this period, all associated data is irrecoverable.
