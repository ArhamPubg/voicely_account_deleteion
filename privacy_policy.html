Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Welcome to Voicely! To provide you with seamless Voice over IP (VoIP) services, we collect and process the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Phone numbers, usernames, and authentication data used for account registration and login via Firebase Authentication.
  • Contacts List: With your explicit permission, we access your device's contact list to help you find and connect with friends who also use Voicely.
  • Call Metadata: Logs of incoming and outgoing calls, including timestamps, call duration, and participant IDs, which are securely stored in Firebase Firestore to power your call history.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, and FCM (Firebase Cloud Messaging) tokens to deliver push notifications for incoming calls.

2. How We Use Your Information

We strictly use your data to operate, maintain, and improve the Voicely application. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Establish secure audio connections between users.
  • Notify you of incoming calls even when the app is in the background or terminated.
  • Provide a unified call history log.
  • Ensure app stability by analyzing crash reports and performance analytics.

3. Data Security & Call Privacy

Your privacy is our highest priority. We employ industry-standard encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest.

Audio streams during live calls are handled dynamically through LiveKit WebRTC infrastructure and are encrypted in transit. We do not record, store, or monitor your audio conversations on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Voicely relies on trusted third-party infrastructure to function securely. These providers are bound by strict data processing agreements:

  • Firebase (Google): Used for authentication, Firestore database, crashlytics, analytics, and push notifications.
  • LiveKit: Used to establish and maintain real-time low-latency audio transmission via WebRTC.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You retain full control over your personal data. You have the right to:

  • Revoke microphone, contacts, or notification permissions at any time via your device settings.
  • Request the deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting our support team or using the in-app deletion feature.
  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
